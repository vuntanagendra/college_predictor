Explore Career Paths & Exams After Class 12

Find the right path with entrance exams, career options, and college guides

Science (with Mathematics)

  • Careers: Engineering, Architecture, Defense, Data Science, Robotics
  • Exams: JEE Main & Advanced, BITSAT, VITEEE, NDA, ISRO Entrance
  • Courses: B.Tech, B.Sc, B.Arch, Integrated M.Sc

Science (with Biology)

  • Careers: Doctor, Pharmacist, Research Scientist, Biotechnologist
  • Exams: NEET, AIIMS, JIPMER, CUET for B.Sc
  • Courses: MBBS, BDS, BPT, B.Sc Nursing, Biotechnology

Commerce

  • Careers: Accountant, Financial Analyst, CA, CS, Marketing Executive
  • Exams: CUET, IPMAT, CA Foundation, CS Foundation, DU JAT
  • Courses: B.Com, BBA, BMS, CA, CS, Economics Hons

Arts / Humanities

  • Careers: Civil Services, Psychologist, Journalist, Writer, Social Worker
  • Exams: CUET, UPSC (later), DU Entrance, SET, TISS BAT
  • Courses: B.A. in Psychology, History, Literature, Political Science

Other Career Paths

  • Design: NID, NIFT, UCEED exams for B.Des
  • Hotel Management: NCHMCT JEE, IIHM
  • Law: CLAT, AILET, SLAT
  • Animation & VFX, Aviation, Language & Linguistics
